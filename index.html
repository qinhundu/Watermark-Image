What is a watermark image?

A watermark image is a visible overlay — typically text, a logo, or a pattern — placed on top of a photo or graphic to identify the owner and prevent unauthorized use. When you watermark image, you add a semi-transparent mark that stays visible while keeping the underlying content clear. Photographers, designers, and businesses use watermark image techniques to protect their creative work online.
